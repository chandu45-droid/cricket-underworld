# Playtesting Feedback — Round 1

**Date:** 2026-06-06
**Status:** Pending implementation

---

## Critical (breaks the core loop)

- **Match too easy** — outcome feels predetermined, player will win anyway. Needs real difficulty and variance.
- **Batting/bowling unclear** — during the gameplay screen, it's not clear whether you are batting or bowling.
- **Coins useless** — too many coins accumulate with nowhere meaningful to spend them. Coin sinks not active.
- **Alignment 100 = no heat = dead game** — if alignment is fully positive, heat never triggers and the mafia/corruption systems become inaccessible. Contradicts the core game concept. Need heat from external sources (rival exposure, media) not just self-inflicted corruption.

## Core Missing Features

- **Mafia/bribe screens not showing** — the corruption offer flow is not surfacing in gameplay.
- **Squad selection + captain choice** — no pre-match squad picking or captain assignment.
- **Scorecard** — no match scorecard shown after games.
- **Powerplay restrictions** — powerplay field restrictions not enforced or visible in match.
- **Player sell/release** — squad capped at 15, no way to remove or sell unwanted players.

## New Systems to Build

- **Trading window** — allow players to trade/buy/sell players with rival managers.
- **Academy + Scout setup** — recruit and develop players through academy and scouting pipelines.
- **Player training** — assign players to training sessions to improve stats.
- **Knockout/playoff bracket** — structured elimination format for end-of-season playoffs.
- **Season stats leaderboard** — top performers per season: batting, bowling, MVP.
- **Social media + controversies** — manage campaigns, social media presence, controversies arising from them.
- **Player ban scenarios** — players getting banned (doping, conduct, corruption exposure).

## Priority Order (agreed)

1. Match difficulty + batting/bowling clarity + scorecard
2. Mafia screens + squad/captain selection (core identity of the game)
3. Coin sinks + player sell/trade (economy feels meaningful)
4. Alignment/heat rework — heat must be reachable on the clean path too
5. Academy, training, knockout bracket, social media, player bans
