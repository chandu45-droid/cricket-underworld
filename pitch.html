The Product

Strategy game. Cricket skin. Mafia twist.

Cricket Underworld combines IPL-style player auctions, squad management, and auto-simulated matches — with a corruption alignment system that gives every decision moral weight. Think Football Manager meets GTA's underworld, built for 5-minute mobile sessions.

⚔

IPL-Style Auctions

Real-time bidding against AI rivals with personalities. Budget management, bluff detection, strategic patience. Every auction is a power play.

♟

Squad Strategy

50 unique player cards with stats, morale, and form. Choose batting order, bowling plans, field settings. Your strategy shapes outcomes.

⚙

Auto-Sim Matches

Matches simulate ball-by-ball based on your strategy + player stats. Watch momentum shift, trigger tactical hooks mid-match. No twitch reflexes.

Alignment System

Accept mafia offers for short-term power. Risk investigations, bans, rival leaks. Play clean for shields and reputation. Every choice has consequences.

Fixer Economy

Hire staff like PR Managers, Lawyers, Cleaners, and Inside Men. Each unlocks new strategic options. Build your empire — clean or corrupt.

What Makes It Different

The alignment spectrum

Every other cricket game asks "how well can you bat?" We ask: "How far will you go to win?" Alignment isn't binary — it's a spectrum from -100 (corrupt) to +100 (clean). Your position unlocks different strategies, staff, and story paths.
