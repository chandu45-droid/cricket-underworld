------------------------- PROMPT: HUB --------------------------------

You are a senior mobile game UI/visual designer. Design ONE screen — the HOME HUB —
for a premium mobile game called "Cricket Underworld."

THE GAME (mood): A cricket-auction + squad-building strategy game set in an Indian
underworld. Think IPL auction broadcast glamour crossed with Mumbai crime-noir
(Sacred Games energy). The player is a team owner building a cricket EMPIRE while
navigating mafia, police, politicians and street dons. Core fantasy: "I am getting
RICH and POWERFUL, and I want to come back tomorrow."

WHY I'M BRIEFING YOU: My current design scored 1/10 with me. Its failure was that it
was just flat dark-blue glass PANELS with numbers on them — UI chrome, not desire.
Do NOT give me tidy panels. I want a category jump.

THE 5 THINGS THE DESIGN MUST DELIVER (this is the whole brief):
1. ART & WORLD — a lit, deep, atmospheric scene, not flat glass. Floodlit-stadium
   meets crime-noir. Real depth, rim lighting, a light source, a sense of place.
2. MATERIAL — surfaces look physical and expensive: brushed metal, gold foil,
   embossed edges, faceted gemstone-like accents. Nothing looks like a plain rectangle.
3. A HERO MOMENT — one dominant focal element the eye lands on first: the player's
   EMPIRE NET-WORTH, staged like a trophy, with the money in SOLID GOLD.
4. MONEY THAT FEELS EXPENSIVE — currency is the star, presented like it's climbing /
   just went up. Big, gold, celebratory — not a quiet grey number.
5. GOLD AUSTERITY — solid gold appears on ONLY two things (the hero net-worth number
   and the primary action button). Everywhere else uses restraint so the gold reads as
   valuable by contrast. Do not spray gold at 10% opacity across every panel.

CONTENT THAT MUST APPEAR ON THE HUB:
- HERO: "Your Empire" net-worth number in Rupees (e.g. Rs 4,20,000), a Rank (#1), and an
  escalating title (Gully Cricketer -> City Baron -> National Don -> Cricket Emperor),
  with a progress bar to the next title.
- Three currencies in the top bar: Coins (gold), Gems, and Black Money (shady cash).
- A DAILY STREAK strip: Day 1->7 escalating rewards, a flame/streak counter, a
  "don't break your streak" pull.
- A NEXT-RIVAL battle card with a STAKE ("Beat the Deccan Sharks or drop to Rank #4"),
  a menacing rival boss portrait, and a primary CTA.
- A "Power Web" glance at 5 underworld factions the player is entangled with
  (Syndicate / Police / Politician / Street Don / Rival Bosses) — status at a glance.
- Primary action button: START AUCTION (the money-making core loop) — solid gold.
- A bottom navigation bar.

NON-NEGOTIABLE IDENTITY:
- ANGULAR design language: sharp 3-4px corners, clip-path facets, knife-edge geometry.
  NO rounded cards. (Sharp = premium/dangerous, fits the underworld tone — like a cut
  gem or a blade, not soft bubbly UI.)
- FICTIONAL everything: no real cricketer names, faces, or likenesses. Any player
  figure must be an original illustrated archetype/silhouette, never a real person.
- India-first, mobile portrait, single screen.

REFERENCES FOR THE CEILING (match this level of craft): EA Sports FC Ultimate Team
card material & rarity, IPL auction broadcast presentation, Dream11's polish,
crime-noir color grading.

OUTPUT: A single self-contained HTML file with inline CSS (no frameworks, no external
assets except Google Fonts) rendering a HIGH-FIDELITY static mockup of this Hub at
390x844 (portrait, DPR 2). Use CSS gradients, shadows, clip-paths and layered elements
to fake the lighting, metal, and depth. Make it look like a $10M game's home screen.
Show me the finished screen — no explanation needed.
